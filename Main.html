HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

WASD / Arrow Keys Move Your Ship
SPACE Fire (Keyboard Mode) / Bomb (Mouse Mode)
SHIFT Activate Bomb Ability
Q / Right Click Fire Missile
P Pause Game

ABILITIES

Extinction Forcefield (Bomb)

Ultimate spell bomb, destroys everything in your path. Use it wisely.

Homing Missiles

Lock-on missiles that track and destroy enemies.

Triangulation Laser

Temporary powerup that adds angled spread shots to your main weapon for more range.

Extra Life

Rare pickup that grants an additional life. Drops occasionally from MiniBoss enemies.

CHEAT CODES

For testing and experimentation, but also for you to have fun:
